Trust the engine

Built for firms that hold client money & data.

AES-256 at rest · TLS 1.2+ Per-user OAuth · full audit log Stripe (PCI) payments SOC 2 · in progress
Is my clients' data safe?

Every firm is fully isolated on its own subdomain with query-level tenant separation. Data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit, payments run through Stripe (PCI), and every action — human or AI — is logged.

Will it really look like my brand?

Yes. Your logo, colors, portal title and subdomain are applied live in the sign-up wizard by the same theming engine that renders your production portal. Your clients never see FirmFlow.

Do I have to rip out QuickBooks?

No — FirmFlow syncs two-way with QuickBooks Online. Invoices, payments and receivables stay in step automatically.

What can the AI actually do today?

Right now the AI directs document-collection and tax-organizer workflows end to end, with a human-confirm step before it acts. Invoicing, payment and bank-rec commands are on the near-term roadmap.

How long does setup take?

Branded and live in an afternoon — no IT project, no six-week onboarding. Import your clients and connect QuickBooks, and the automations start running.
